A Grand Father has the winsdom of long experience and the love of an understanding heart .

Grand Fathers are unique members of our families, not everyone is lucky enough to have met their grandfather, much less share so many fun and loving memories with them. I was so blessed as a child, having grandparents to dote on me. Spoiled was an understatement. Not with things but rather with experience, and wealth of knowledge that only they could share with me. We lived all together in a big country house in the suburbs of a province in Italy. My Grand Father was running our farm, he grew up really poor and built that from scratch along with acres of vineyards. I became president and producer of a Parmigiano Reggiano distributor and producer in collaboration with Cantine Riunite Lambrusco. I would often sit by the porch with him and talk for hours. His lifestory was inspiring and everyday he would come out with a precious new life advise that I would always keep in consideration. He was one of the most brilliant people I've ever met,his charisma was magnetic and he was also a very funny guy!This little page is just to say "Thank you" to a very dear person Who taught me life and I miss dearly.
Love you GrandPa!
